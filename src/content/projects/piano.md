**Piano** is a free web practice studio for learning **piano and electric guitar** — with the goal of musical expression and the ability to play by ear. Every time you open it, it tells you exactly what to do next and keeps a sharp record of what you've learned, so practice never stalls on *"wait, what should I do?"* or *"what was that one thing I forgot?"*

## The idea
It deletes two sentences from practice: *"wait, what should I do?"* and *"shit, I forgot that one thing — what was it again?"*

Inside a session, the **stand** is tonight's plan — what to do now, what's next, when you're done. On the roadmap, **Your Path** (a skill tree) shows what you've learned, the one thing to learn next, and the way back to anything you forgot.

## Features
- 🎯 **Exact next action** – Fundamentals-first lessons; never wonder what to practise
- 🌳 **Skill tree** – A visual map of what you know and what's next
- 🎹 **Interactive notation** – Live keyboard/fretboard diagrams, sheet music, chord shapes, and playable audio
- 📖 **Beginner-proof** – Every musical term stays tappable, so a total beginner can follow any lesson cold
- 💾 **Yours** – Client-side and localStorage-first, with optional signed-in cloud sync
- 🆓 **Free & open** – Honest numbers, real teaching, exact next actions — nothing else

Built on Next.js 16 / React 19 with Tone.js for audio and VexFlow for notation.

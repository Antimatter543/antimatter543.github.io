**Bilateral** is a free, open-source web tool for EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) bilateral stimulation. Follow a moving dot with your eyes while holding a thought loosely in mind — the working memory competition helps reduce emotional intensity of distressing memories or feelings.

## Features
- **Visual stimulation** – Sine-wave pendulum motion with natural deceleration at edges
- **Audio stimulation** – Stereo bilateral tones (sine, click, rain) panning left-to-right
- **Haptic stimulation** – Device vibration for mobile
- **Timed sessions** – Configurable duration, rest periods, and number of sets
- **Fully customisable** – Dot size, colour palette, speed, frequency, volume
- **Zero dependencies** – Pure vanilla JS/CSS/HTML, no frameworks
- **Privacy-first** – No account, no tracking, no paywall. Preferences saved locally.
